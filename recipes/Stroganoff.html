Stroganoff Recipe

How to make Stroganoff

A deliciously rich and creamy beef stroganoff that's easy to prep in a slow cooker with little-to-no fuss. It smells wonderful and is tasty like no other stroganoff you've had before thanks to the addition of cream cheese along with the sour cream. Serve over hot, buttered flat noodles.

Stroganoff Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Canned Soup
  • Seasonings
  • Herbs
  • Wine
  • Cornstarch and Flour
  • Sour Cream and Cream Cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Additional Ingredients: Serve with noodles or white rice(My prefrence)

Steps to create your masterpiece

  1. Spread beef stew meat into bottom of slow cooker
  2. Whisk wine,cornstarch,flour in small bowl then pour in mixture
  3. Cook on low for 6 hours 7. Stir sour cream,mushrooms,cream cheese, and parsley into mixture
  4. Serve over pasta
